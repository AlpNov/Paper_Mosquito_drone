Reviewer 3 of CASE, ISAM 2016 submission 10

Comments to the author
======================

The paper presents a contribution in the filed of drone
application. Despite the idea is quite interesting,
unfortunately the scientific matter is very limited, and
the experiments are very limited. My suggestion is to spend
more efforts in developing the experiments. 

Comments on the Video
=====================

The quality of the video is average, the tables are well
descriptive and clear.
